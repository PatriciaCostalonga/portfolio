About me

I graduated in Design and have worked in many different industries. Being a Designer and having constant contact with new technologies, made me want to dive deeper into the tech world. I took the leap of courage last year and decided to make a big career change and step up my game by learning how to code. I started with C language to understand the logic of coding, but it was in JavaScript combined with CSS and HTML where I found my true passion.

People are more than tools. They are a collection of values and skills. To learn how to use a tool isn’t hard, but to understand what you can achieve, it’s what it will get you to the next level. I want to share my ideas and best practices on how to improve business and to truly connect with a customer. In exchange, I hope that I can mature professionally there and learn a lot about the process.
