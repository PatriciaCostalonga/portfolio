About me

Hello, my name is Patricia Costalonga! Thank you for investing your precious time in taking a look at my portfolio.

I’m an Industrial Designer who is fascinated with web development. In 2019 I decided to please my curiosity and learn how to code to become part of the digital world too. Since then, I've been studying a lot and having fun with my discoveries as well. The digital world amazes me!

I proudly come from Rio de Janeiro, one of the most beautiful and intense cities in Brazil. Right now, I’m based in London with my husband and loving my experience of living in this huge cosmopolitan city.

My passion for Design can be explained by my empathic builder's mindset and my strong creative vein. I'm a good listener who enjoys understanding people's needs and developing meaningful solutions.
